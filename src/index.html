Playground

Welcome to your gluebert.js playground. Test, try-out - hassle free just working out of the box.

Don't forget to checkout the Documentation if you have any questions or file an issue/enhancement at the github-repository

Open your console

Although the output is not pretty impressive, you will see the lazy-loading in the network panel in the console.

In this example repository you can find some example modules at `/src/modules/`
